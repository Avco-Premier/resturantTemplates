Welcome to Pizzeria Italia!

This is our first store on Long Island, and we couldn't be more excited to be here. We've been serving New York City for the last 28 years, carrying on a tradition that began when my father opened his first pizzeria in Queens in 1977.

I've been making pizza the same way my father made it—and now I'm passing that same tradition to my sons. We offer a full arrangement of Italian dishes and pizza, prepared with the same care and recipes that have made our family proud for three generations.

We deliver, and we also cater for any occasion. We look forward to becoming a part of this community and filling your bellies with good, wholesome Italian food—because here at Pizzeria Italia, we're bringing the city to Long Island.
